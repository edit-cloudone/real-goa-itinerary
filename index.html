Monsoon Goa

Day 1 – Bangalore to Goa & Baga Beach

The journey started from Bangalore early in the morning. The scenic drive through the Western Ghats was filled with lush greenery and monsoon clouds.

Reaching Goa by afternoon, the first destination was the famous Baga Beach. The cool sea breeze, beach shacks, and lively atmosphere made for a perfect introduction to Goa.

Day 2 – Calangute & Candolim Exploration

Day two was dedicated to North Goa's beautiful coastline. Starting with Calangute Beach, often called the "Queen of Beaches", followed by a peaceful evening at Candolim.

The monsoon weather added a magical touch with dramatic skies, fewer crowds, and endless photo opportunities.

Day 3 – Panjim City Tour & Return to Bangalore

The final day was spent exploring Panjim, the charming capital city of Goa. Portuguese-style architecture, colorful streets, and the Mandovi River waterfront offered a completely different experience from the beaches.

After lunch, it was time to begin the return journey to Bangalore, carrying unforgettable memories of Goa's beaches, culture, and monsoon beauty.
